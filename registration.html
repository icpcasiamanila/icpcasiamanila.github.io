How to Register

  1. Register your team on the ICPC Web Site.
  2. Pay the registration fee of your team.
  3. You will receive a confirmation regarding your registration through your ICPC account.

All contestants, coaches, and guests must be fully vaccinated against COVID-19.

Registration Period

  • Registration opens on 10 October 2025
  • Registration closes on 10 November 2025

Registration Fee

  • Foreign teams: USD 250
    • Each extra person: USD 50
  • Local (Philippine) teams: PHP 12,000
    • Each extra person: PHP 2,500

Extra persons may include co-coaches, observers, guests, parents, etc.

Registration Inclusions

Team registration covers three (3) contestants and one (1) coach. The registration fee includes:

  • ICPC Manila T-Shirt
  • Contest Materials
  • Meals
    • First Day: lunch, snacks
    • Second Day: breakfast, lunch, dinner

The registration fee does not include hotel accomodations and transportation

Payment Instructions

You will be issued an official receipt during on-site registration

No refund will be made for cancellation of registration for whatever reason

For Payment in USD

Online Payment via Credit Card

This online payment facility accepts Visa / MasterCard Debit or Credit Card of any issuing bank.

Please read and follow the instructions below.

  1. Please provide the following information on the payment form:
    • Reference Number: 225078004
    • Amount to be Paid (USD 250 per team, plus USD 50 per extra person if any)
    • Payer Name (name to be indicated in the official receipt)
    • Particulars: ICPC Manila 2025 Registration
    • Nominated E-Mail Address (your email address where the payment confirmation will be sent)
  2. Select the preferred payment method.
  3. Enter the card details.
    • Card Number
    • Expiry Date (month and year)
    • Security Code / CVV (3 digits at the end of the signature panel of your card)
  4. Wait for the payment to be processed. Confirmation will be sent to your nominated e-mail address.
  5. The organizers will receive the bank confirmation after at least three (3) banking days.

Payment through Bank Wire Transfer

Please read and follow the instructions below.
  1. Go to the nearest/most convenient bank in your area.
  2. Deposit your payment in US Dollars via wire transfer to: Bank of the Philippine Islands [USD]
    • Account Name: Ateneo de Manila University, Inc.
    • USD Account Number: 3084-0002-04
    • SWIFT Code: BOPIPHMM
    • Branch Code: BR00308
    • Reference / Info / Comment / Note: 225078004
  3. Make sure to have a copy of the validated transaction document.
  4. As soon as payment is made, send a scanned copy of the validated transaction document to icpc.discs@ateneo.edu with the following details:
    • University Name
    • Team Name
    • Names of Team Members
  5. The organizers will receive the bank confirmation after at least three (3) banking days.
